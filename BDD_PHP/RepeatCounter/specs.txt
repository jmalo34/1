SPEC 1
A word is found in a string
Input:  cookie; Seven batches of cookies were baked.
Output:  1

SPEC 2
Partial matches are not counted
Input: cookie; There were seven types of cookies on the cookie platter.
Output: 1

SPEC 3
The string is not case-sensitive
Input:  cookie; Cookie World sold seven types of cookies at each of their cookie stores.
Output:  2

SPEC 4
Non-alphabetic characters are ignored
Input: ball; One of the most commonly heard phrases in baseball, "Play ball!", is shouted immediately prior to the ball game commencing.
Output:  2
